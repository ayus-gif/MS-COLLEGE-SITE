Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

What is BCA?

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) is a 3-year undergraduate degree program focused on Computer Science, Programming, Software Development and Information Technology.

Why Choose BCA?

BCA is ideal for students interested in computers, software development, web technologies and modern IT careers.

Eligibility

Students who have completed 10+2 from a recognized board are eligible for admission to the BCA course.

Duration

BCA is a 3-year degree program divided into 6 semesters with practical and theoretical learning.

Objectives of BCA

Develop programming skills, logical thinking, problem-solving abilities and knowledge of modern computer technologies.
