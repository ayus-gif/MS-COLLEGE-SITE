About MS College Motihari

Munshi Singh College, Motihari was established in 1945 and is a constituent unit of B.R.A. Bihar University, Muzaffarpur. The college is one of the leading institutions of higher education in East Champaran and offers various undergraduate and vocational courses including BCA.
